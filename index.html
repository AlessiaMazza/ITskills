HOMEPAGE

Our group is composed of six people:
- Rossella Verolino and Giovanna De Felice worked on a story about the piano and the most productive pianist.
- Elena Benelli and Domiziana Leorin worked on a story about the violin and the most influential violinists.
- Alessia Mazza and Veronica Spena worked on a story about a famous Italian composer and the museum dedicated to him.
Throughout the stories, you will discover more details about these musical instruments.
Moreover, you will explore these musicians' and composers' personal and professional backgrounds.
